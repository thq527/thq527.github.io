✦About

I am an Associate Research Fellow at Zhejiang Gongshang University. My research focuses on network resource allocation and routing optimization, with particular interests in computing networks and programmable networks.

In the last five years, I have published more than 20 papers, with 15 first-author or corresponding-author papers. My work has appeared in venues such as IEEE/ACM Transactions on Networking, IEEE Transactions on Parallel and Distributed Systems, IEEE Transactions on Cloud Computing, and Computer Networks.
